🍎 Healthy Food Recipes

Avocado Toast 🥑

Toast whole-grain bread, top with mashed avocado, add salt & pepper.

Oatmeal Bowl 🍚

Cook oats with milk, add honey, nuts, and fresh fruits.

Green Smoothie 🍃

Blend spinach, banana, yogurt, and chia seeds for a healthy smoothie.

Greek Salad 🥗

Mix cucumber, tomatoes, olives, and feta cheese. Drizzle with olive oil.

Quinoa Bowl 🍚

Cook quinoa and add grilled chicken, spinach, and lemon dressing.

Grilled Salmon 🐟

Grill salmon with lemon, garlic, and herbs. Serve with steamed veggies.

Fruit Yogurt 🍓

Mix Greek yogurt with fresh berries and a drizzle of honey.

Chia Pudding 🍮

Soak chia seeds in almond milk overnight. Top with fruits.

Banana & Oatmeal Protein Shake

Rich in fiber and protein, this shake is perfect for a morning boost or post-workout recovery.
